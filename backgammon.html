Backgammon

How To Play

  1. Move your pieces in the direction shown.
  2. The winner is the first to move all of their pieces to their home trough.
  3. You may only move pieces to the home trough if they are all in the home quarter (the one closest to the home trough).
  4. Move pieces by selecting a valid piece, then a valid die (if a piece or die is valid, it will be highlighted).
  5. You may not move to a space occupied by 2 or more of your opponent's pieces.
  6. Moving to a space occupied by 1 enemy piece will result in it being captured.
  7. Captured pieces must be brought back before you can make further moves.
  8. Captured pieces are brought back in the opposing home quarter.
  9. Rolling a double allows each die to be used twice. This is represented by 4 dice.
